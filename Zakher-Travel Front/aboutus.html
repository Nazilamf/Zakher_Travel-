Who We Are?

Zakher Travel officially started its activity in 2016 and soon managed to make an outstanding achievement. It received thousands of tourists from the GCC region and other countries and began to introduce them the charming treasures not only Azerbaijan’s , but also countries’ such as Russia, Kazakhstan , Kyrgyzstan, Poland, Ukraine and UAE. For two years of our existence, we have opened brunches in these countries. We are a tour operator and we have a contract with more than 500 travel companies. We provide tailor made holidays to our customers with our expert staff consisting of 100 and more people and professional services.
